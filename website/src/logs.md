They are of the form `abbcd`.
* `a` - General category of a page.

    * `1` are homework pages
    * `2` are note pages
    * `3` are exam solution pages
* `bb` - Item entry; for homeworks, these are labeled by weeks.
* `c` - Action type, either `A` or `B`.
