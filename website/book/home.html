Algebra II Homeworks

Overview

Course Description

This site is used for holding the homework, sample solution files for the course Algebra II taught by Prof. Wu-Yen Chuang at NTU (National Taiwan University).

This semester's topic is on module theory, commutative algebra, homological algebra and the theory of group representations.

References

The course will mainly use the following books:

  • [AtM] Atiyah, M. (2018). Introduction to commutative algebra. CRC Press.
  • [GM] Gelfand, S. I., & Manin, Y. I. (2013). Methods of homological algebra. Springer Science & Business Media.
  • [Ser] Serre, J. P. (1977). Linear representations of finite groups (Vol. 42). New York: springer.
  • [Lang] Lang, S. (2012). Algebra (Vol. 211). Springer Science & Business Media.
  • [DF] Dummit, D. S., & Foote, R. M. (2004). Abstract algebra (Vol. 3). Hoboken: Wiley.
  • [FH], Fulton and Harris, Representation Theory: A First Course, Vol. 129. Springer Science & Business Media, 2013.

Other supplementary notes are given along the way as the course unfolds.
